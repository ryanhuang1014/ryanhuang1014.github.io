About me

I received the B.Eng. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China, in 2015. Later, I received the Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Yunjie Liu and Prof. Jiao Zhang from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications , Beijing, China, in 2021. From 2021 to 2023, I was a postdoctoral researcher with Purple Mountain Laboratories, Nanjing, China. I am currently an assistant researcher with the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University.
